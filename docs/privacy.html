Privacy Policy — MagicCut BG

Last updated: 2025-10-31

MagicCut BG (“App”) helps you remove image backgrounds.

What we process

  • Images you choose in the App. Images are processed locally and/or via a local proxy at http://localhost that you control.
  • The App does not sell personal data.

How processing works

  • The desktop App sends your selected image to a local proxy (default http://localhost:5055) for background removal.
  • Depending on your proxy/configuration, processing may occur locally or call a third‑party service you control.
  • The App itself does not upload your images to our servers.

Local data

  • Minimal settings (e.g., trials counter, subscription flag, proxy path) stored under your user profile’s AppData\Local\MagicCutBG.

Analytics

No analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.

Your choices

  • Delete the App’s local settings by removing the MagicCutBG folder in %LocalAppData%.

Children

Not directed to children under 13.
